About Us

Established in 2012 at Bantul, Special Region of Yogyakarta - Indonesia. Indo Risakti specializes in environmentally friendly products including Basketry, Boxes, Wall Art, Mirrors, and Decorative items. We develop and produce products from natural materials and recycled materials such as water hyacinth, seagrass, banana bark, mendong and recycle wood which is considered as “green” material, and also old newspapers/magazines, and any cast-off materials. We work together with local craftsmen and the community to achieve mutual benefit which also gives the opportunity to grow them in a sustainable trading environment. With Indonesia ' s abundant cultural diversity, we continue to explore traditional craftsmanship to produce premium home decorative handicraft that complements contemporary living. We are experienced in volume production and international logistics (Europe and USA) with emphasis on quality, design and timely shipment

Our Product

Deliver natural into your interior

Sustainable, handmade home decor is a growing trend that combines the beauty of natural materials with ethical and eco-friendly practices. By choosing handcrafted pieces made from sustainable resources, you can create a unique and personalized living space that reflects your commitment to environmental responsibility. These items often feature intricate details and unique designs, showcasing the skill and artistry of the craftspeople involved. Not only are they aesthetically pleasing, but sustainable, handmade home decor also supports local communities and reduces the environmental impact of mass-produced products
