About Us

Indo Risakti is a leading provider of sustainable and ethically sourced Indonesian handicrafts.
Our mission is to empower local artisans and promote Indonesian culture through beautiful, handmade products.
Founded in 2012 by Riris Simanjuntak and Windu Sinaga, we have grown into a thriving business with a global reach. Our team of passionate individuals is committed to delivering exceptional quality and customer service. We believe in the power of fair trade and the positive impact it can have on communities. Discover the beauty of Indonesian craftsmanship at Indo Risakti.

Our Location

Our company is located in the serene Bantul district of Yogyakarta, offering a peaceful and authentic experience. Conveniently situated near ancient temples and cultural attractions, we are well-connected to the vibrant city center. Additionally, we are easily accessible from Yogyakarta International Airport (YIA), making it convenient for both domestic and international travelers to visit our facilities. Moreover, our location provides us with easy access to a network of skilled craftsmen and artisans, allowing us to source high-quality materials and collaborate on unique projects.
